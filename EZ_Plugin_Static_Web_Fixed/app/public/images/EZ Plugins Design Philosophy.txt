Introduction/design philosophy:
I am Symest, a music producer looking to develop a series of VST3 effect plugins designed to be beginner friendly, and exceptionally powerful, all aptly named "EZ [plugin name, e.g. EZ eq. EZ compressor, EZ multiband]". They are simple to understand, intuitive to use, optimized for budget hardware, and pretty to look at. They are completely free, and seen as alternatives to the big expensive plugins, the ones virtually every bedroom producer just pirates like FabFilter, Zynaptiq PITCHMAP, etc. Anyone can download these and have easy to use, professional tools. Virtually every knob or setting has a small "?" icon next to it, that explains the function in a simple to understand way, and how it's commonly used. Every plugin has the same color palette, the same font, and an advanced preset system with a picker, importer, and exporter, with presets formatted in .json for simplicity and ease of editing/access. The plugins are compatible with virtually any DAW with VST3 support. It's made for everyone: beginners, pros who want something free and easy, minimalists, maximalists, etc. Every effect plugin has a delta, bypass, and a/b switcher for convenience and so you can hear the DIFFERENCE. The UI version installer comes with a little app that lets you customize your theme universally as well, allowing for very good personalization. But ever better, Since the plugins' UI are card based, you can move around the UI however you want, and it remembers how you last saved it upon relaunching.
